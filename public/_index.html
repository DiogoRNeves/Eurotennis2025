Welcome to Eurotennis 2025

Join us for an exceptional tennis experience in the heart of Vilamoura. Four exciting tournaments featuring the best amateur tennis players from across Europe.

Tournament Format

Singles Tournament

  • • 16 players per category
  • • 4 groups in group phase
  • • Best of 3 short sets (tie break at 4-all)
  • • Match tie break in 3rd set
  • • No-advantage scoring

Doubles Tournament

  • • 24 pairs per category
  • • 8 groups in group phase
  • • Best of 3 short sets (tie break at 3-all)
  • • Match tie break in 3rd set
  • • No-advantage scoring
